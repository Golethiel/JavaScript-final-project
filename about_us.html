About Us



The Travel Guide started out as just one man, James Fletcher. He was very familiar with the airline companies, and he was the go-to guy for booking affordable flights and finding deals in hotels. The company has expanded on his simple vision of doing the hard part of vacation, the planning, and turning it into a platform that anybody can use. What is your vacation destination? That is and will be our vision for many years.
